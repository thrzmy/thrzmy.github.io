LCIS

The Locational Clearance Information System (LCIS) represents a sophisticated solution dedicated to the modernization and optimization of Locational Clearance application management within the municipality. Through the strategic implementation of automation, LCIS aims to significantly enhance the efficiency of record creation, monitoring, and retrieval, ensuring both expeditious and accurate outcomes.

The system is designed to facilitate digitalization, allowing for remote access to locational plans and strategically decreasing reliance on conventional paper documentation within the confines of the Municipal Planning and Development Coordinator's office. LCIS establishes a centralized data repository, thereby streamlining processes and expediting assessments, ultimately offering a more systematic and efficient approach to the management of locational clearances.
